About me

I was born on the 12 October 1994 in Lima, Peru. I attended one of the biggest and most prestigious schools in Peru, Colegio de La Inmaculada . An institution focused on instilling values and forming well rounded men that look out for the well-being of their community and have a hunger for knowledge. After completing 9 years of education, I switched schools in order to complete the International Baccalaureate Diploma at San Ignacio de Recalde School on my final two years of high-school finishing top quarter of my year.

After finishing high-school I decided to pursue my higher education in England knowing that this decision would mean being able to get high quality education, learn a lot of valuable life skills and gain more independence. During 2012-2013, I went to the university of Bristol to do an IFP English with Physics and Mathematics course. Following this first experience I moved on to the University of Essex on 2013 to start a 3 year Bsc. Computer Science degree.

The undergraduate curriculum in Computer Science at the University of Essex provided me with a good understanding of fundamental concepts in the field and I believe has equipped me with valuable software design and development skills across a wide range of subjects. I learned these by taking various modules such as: Professional development, Object-Oriented programming, Web development, Introduction to Databases, Network Fundamentals, Software engineering, Application programming, Data structures and algorithms, Human computer interfaces and visualisation, Web application programming and Advanced programming among others. On my final year at essex I worked on a program for the rendering of procedurally generated cities, using PyOpenGL and also worked for the university as a Resident Assistant looking after the wellbeing of new students.

Along my studies, I have found a prime interest in web technologies and the possibilities that internet applications offer. I am fascinated by the profound impact on society and scale of some of the world leading companies in the field e.g. Amazon, Facebook, Google, Netflix, etc. and would like to be part of the revolution, and perhaps even one day begin my own online business. I have learned the importance of interface design and enjoy the process of optimising applications for a pleasant user experience. In addition to this, my time working as a web development assistant at the Student's Union cemented my decision that this was the field I wanted to specialize in and pursue a career in the future.

I am currently studying a masters in Advanced Web Engineering until summer of 2018.

I lived all my life in Peru before moving to England in 2012 to start University. My dream is to one day be able to combine all my interests together, using all the knowledge and skills I am acquiring at university.
